We are Cultist Empire.

Welcome to Wild West.

Cultist Empire is one of CCNet's largest nations, with over 200 active residents. Founded in September 2022, CE spans from the heights of the Canadian Rockies to the depths of the Amazon Rainforest to the barren deserts of New Mexico, encompassing a wide variety of diverse environments perfect for any players looking to settle.

What even is CCNet?

The best server you've seen in a while.

CCNet is a one-of-a-kind Minecraft multiplayer experience where settlers build towns and nations wage war. Players can design, manufacture and pilot their own vehicles and war machines, and can engage in intense battles with functional guns, in addition to vanilla potion PvP. Non-combatants have their fun too — electrical grids power hundreds of different machines, custom plants grow in specific habitats throughout the map, and creativity can be let loose with functional paintings and custom jukeboxes.
